Rizal’s Family, Childhood, and Early Education

Philippine National Hero

Dr. Jose Rizal, the Philippines' national hero, was born on June 19, 1861, in Calamba, Laguna. He grew up in a loving family, being the seventh of eleven children, and showed remarkable intelligence from an early age. Taught first by his mother, Rizal excelled in reading, writing, and poetry, and later pursued formal education in Biñan and at the Ateneo Municipal de Manila, where he graduated with high honors.
