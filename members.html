Group Members - RizalWiki (Group - 3)

Members of the Rizal Study Group

This page showcases the members of Group 3 who worked together on the RizalWiki project. Each member contributed to researching, writing, and compiling information about Jose Rizal's life, family, and legacy. Below is a detailed overview of each group member and their contributions.[1]

Carlos Miguel F. Luna

Carlos Miguel F. Luna
Carlos Miguel F. Luna
Role
Project Leader

Carlos Miguel F. Luna serves as the project leader for Group 3's RizalWiki physical portfolio project. He/She supervised the overall progress of the group, organized the contents of the portfolio, and ensured that all materials were complete and properly compiled. He/She also coordinated with the members to make sure every section met the required standards.

Background

Carlos Miguel F. Luna is a 2nd Year student taking up BSIT at the BPSU. His/Her interest in Philippine history, especially the life and works of Jose Rizal, developed through classroom discussions and research activities. As the team leader, he/she demonstrated strong leadership, responsibility, and teamwork in guiding the group throughout the project.

Role
Layout Design Coordinator and Information Gatherer

Jeush Andrei R. Luna served as the Layout and Design Coordinator for Group 3's RizalWiki physical portfolio project. Shan David Laxamana

Shan David Laxamana
Shan David Laxamana
Role
Materials and Documentation Manager

Shan David Laxamana served as the Materials and Documentation Manager for the project. He handled the printing of documents, gathered the necessary materials, and ensured that all pages were complete before compilation. He also helped in reviewing the content to avoid errors.

Background

Shan is known for being responsible and organized when it comes to tasks. He is attentive to details and makes sure that everything is properly prepared before submission. His reliability helped the group complete the physical portfolio efficiently and on time.

Jho Adrian Hil Macalinao served as the Compilation and Assembly Head for Group 3's RizalWiki physical portfolio. He was in charge of assembling the printed materials, arranging them in correct order, and ensuring the final portfolio was properly secured and presentable.

Eljay Losa

Eljay Losa
Eljay Losa
Role
Website Developer and QR Integration Specialist

Eljay G. Losa served as the Website Developer and QR Integration Specialist for Group 3's RizalWiki physical portfolio project. He was responsible for creating the website version of the project and generating the QR code that was placed in the physical portfolio. Through this, readers can access the digital version of the RizalWiki for a more interactive and accessible experience.

Background

Eljay is an Information Technology student with a strong interest in web development and programming. He has experience working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which he used to design and develop the RizalWiki website. His passion for technology and creativity allowed the group to combine traditional presentation with digital innovation, making the project more engaging and modern.
